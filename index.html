    Adam Nathaniel Gibbs (born June 15, 1986 in Athens, Texas, USA) is an American stage and voice actor. He is known for voicing Hyakkimaru in Dororo, Takashi Natsume in Natsume's Book of Friends, Kuki Urie in Tokyo Ghoul:re, Shinichi Izumi in Parasyte -the maxim-, Seiya Kanie in Amagi Brilliant Park, Uramichi Omota in Life Lessons with Uramichi Oniisan, Hachiman Hikigaya in My Teen Romantic Comedy SNAFU, Taichi Mashima in Chihayafuru, Hōtarō Oreki in Hyouka and Koshi Sugawara in Haikyu!!.
